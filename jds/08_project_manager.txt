Job Title: Senior Project Manager - Construction

Company: BuildRight Construction Group

About BuildRight:
BuildRight Construction Group is one of the largest commercial construction companies in the Southwest, with annual revenues exceeding $800M. We specialize in healthcare facilities, educational institutions, and mixed-use developments. Our portfolio includes over 200 completed projects ranging from $10M to $150M.

Position Overview:
We are seeking an experienced Senior Project Manager to lead large-scale commercial construction projects from pre-construction through closeout. This role requires exceptional leadership, communication, and organizational skills to deliver projects on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards.

Primary Responsibilities:

Project Leadership:
- Manage all aspects of commercial construction projects valued at $30M-$100M
- Lead project teams including assistant project managers, superintendents, and administrative staff
- Serve as primary point of contact for owners, architects, engineers, and stakeholders
- Develop and maintain project master schedules using Primavera P6 or Microsoft Project
- Conduct weekly owner/architect/contractor (OAC) meetings and prepare meeting minutes

Financial Management:
- Prepare and manage project budgets and cash flow projections
- Review and approve subcontractor pay applications
- Process change orders and negotiate with owners and subcontractors
- Prepare monthly cost reports and project financial forecasts
- Monitor job cost accounting and work-in-progress reporting

Pre-Construction:
- Participate in project pursuits including estimates, proposals, and presentations
- Lead subcontractor bidding and buyout process
- Review contract documents and identify scope gaps and risks
- Develop value engineering alternatives

Construction Phase:
- Ensure compliance with contract documents, specifications, and building codes
- Coordinate with superintendents on schedule, safety, and quality
- Manage RFI and submittal processes
- Oversee quality control and punch list completion
- Ensure OSHA compliance and safety program implementation

Project Closeout:
- Coordinate substantial and final completion activities
- Manage warranty and closeout documentation
- Ensure timely receipt of final payments and retention

Required Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Construction Management, Civil Engineering, or Architecture
- Minimum 10 years of commercial construction experience
- 5+ years as Project Manager on projects over $20M
- Experience with healthcare, education, or institutional projects preferred
- Proficiency in construction management software (Procore, Bluebeam, Primavera P6)
- Strong knowledge of construction contracts (AIA, ConsensusDocs)
- Understanding of building codes and permitting processes
- Valid driver's license and ability to visit job sites

Preferred Qualifications:
- Master's degree or MBA
- PMP (Project Management Professional) certification
- LEED AP or other sustainability credentials
- OSHA 30-hour certification
- Experience with design-build and CM-at-Risk delivery methods

Essential Skills:
- Exceptional leadership and team management abilities
- Strong negotiation and conflict resolution skills
- Excellent written and verbal communication
- Detail-oriented with strong organizational skills
- Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously
- Problem-solving mindset with proactive approach

Compensation & Benefits:
- Base Salary: $140,000 - $180,000 depending on experience
- Annual Performance Bonus: 10-20%
- Company Vehicle or Vehicle Allowance
- 401(k) with company match
- Health, Dental, Vision Insurance
- Paid Time Off (4 weeks)
- Professional membership dues (AGC, CMAA, etc.)
- Continuing education support

Location: Phoenix, Arizona with project sites throughout the Southwest region
Travel: 25-40% to project sites
