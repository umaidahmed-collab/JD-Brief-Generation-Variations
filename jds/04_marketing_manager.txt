Job Title: Senior Marketing Manager

About the Company:
GreenLeaf Organics is a fast-growing consumer packaged goods company specializing in organic and sustainable food products. We've grown from a small startup to a $50M revenue company in just 5 years and are now looking to accelerate our growth through strategic marketing initiatives.

About the Role:
We are seeking an experienced Senior Marketing Manager to lead our brand marketing efforts and drive customer acquisition across multiple channels. This role will be responsible for developing and executing comprehensive marketing strategies that increase brand awareness, drive sales, and build customer loyalty.

Key Responsibilities:
- Develop and execute integrated marketing campaigns across digital, social media, retail, and traditional channels
- Manage a marketing budget of $2M+ and optimize spend across channels based on ROI analysis
- Lead a team of 4 marketing professionals including social media specialists and content creators
- Collaborate with product development team to launch 10+ new products annually
- Develop and maintain brand guidelines ensuring consistency across all touchpoints
- Manage relationships with external agencies including creative, media buying, and PR firms
- Analyze market trends, consumer insights, and competitive landscape to inform marketing strategy
- Own the company's social media presence across Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and LinkedIn
- Develop influencer marketing partnerships and ambassador programs
- Create and optimize email marketing campaigns with a focus on customer retention
- Track and report on key marketing KPIs including CAC, LTV, ROAS, and brand awareness metrics
- Coordinate with sales team on retail marketing and in-store promotions

Requirements:
- Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Business Administration, or related field
- 7+ years of marketing experience, with at least 3 years in CPG/FMCG industry
- 3+ years of experience managing direct reports
- Proven track record of developing and executing successful marketing campaigns
- Strong analytical skills with experience in marketing analytics tools (Google Analytics, Tableau, etc.)
- Experience managing marketing budgets of $1M+
- Proficiency in digital marketing including paid social, SEM, and programmatic advertising
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills
- Experience working with creative agencies and managing vendor relationships

Preferred Qualifications:
- MBA or Master's degree in Marketing
- Experience in organic/natural products industry
- Knowledge of retail marketing and trade promotion strategies
- Experience with marketing automation platforms (HubSpot, Marketo, etc.)
- Understanding of sustainability marketing and purpose-driven branding

Benefits:
- Competitive salary: $120,000 - $150,000
- Performance bonus up to 20%
- Equity participation
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance
- 401(k) with company match
- Flexible work arrangements
- Professional development budget
- Employee discount on all products

Location: Denver, Colorado (Hybrid - 3 days in office)
