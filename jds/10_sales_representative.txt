Job Title: Enterprise Sales Representative

Company: CloudSecure Technologies

About CloudSecure:
CloudSecure Technologies is a cybersecurity company providing next-generation cloud security and threat detection solutions. Our platform protects over 500 enterprise customers globally, securing more than $2 trillion in digital assets. We've raised $150M in venture funding and are experiencing 100%+ year-over-year growth.

The Opportunity:
We are looking for a driven Enterprise Sales Representative to join our rapidly growing sales team. In this role, you will be responsible for acquiring new enterprise customers in the Fortune 1000, driving significant revenue growth, and establishing CloudSecure as the trusted cybersecurity partner for large organizations.

Key Responsibilities:

New Business Development:
- Prospect and develop new enterprise accounts with 5,000+ employees
- Build and manage a pipeline of 3-4x annual quota
- Conduct discovery calls to understand customer security challenges
- Deliver compelling product demonstrations and presentations
- Navigate complex enterprise sales cycles (6-12 months)

Sales Execution:
- Achieve quarterly and annual revenue quota ($1.5M-$2M annual target)
- Lead negotiations and close contracts ranging from $200K to $2M+ ACV
- Develop and execute strategic account plans for target accounts
- Coordinate proof-of-concept (POC) and technical evaluations
- Create and present ROI business cases and executive proposals

Relationship Building:
- Build relationships with C-level executives (CISO, CIO, CTO)
- Engage multiple stakeholders including security teams, IT operations, and procurement
- Develop champion relationships within target accounts
- Partner with customer success on reference development

Collaboration:
- Work closely with Sales Engineering on technical requirements
- Coordinate with Marketing on account-based campaigns
- Partner with BDR team on outbound prospecting
- Provide market feedback to Product team
- Participate in industry events and conferences

Required Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree required
- 5-7 years of B2B sales experience with consistent quota achievement
- 3+ years selling enterprise software or technology solutions
- Experience selling to CISO/security organizations strongly preferred
- Track record of closing deals $500K+ ACV
- Experience with complex, multi-stakeholder sales processes
- Proficiency with CRM tools (Salesforce) and sales engagement platforms
- Strong presentation and communication skills
- Ability to travel 30-40% for customer meetings and events

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience in cybersecurity, cloud infrastructure, or enterprise software
- Existing relationships with Fortune 1000 CISOs
- Understanding of cloud security, SIEM, or threat detection markets
- Experience with MEDDIC or similar sales methodologies
- Previous startup or high-growth company experience
- Technical background or ability to understand security concepts

What We're Looking For:
- Hunter mentality with passion for winning new business
- Intellectual curiosity and ability to learn complex solutions
- Resilience and ability to handle rejection
- Competitive drive balanced with team collaboration
- Strong work ethic and self-motivation
- Executive presence and professional demeanor

Compensation Package:
- On-Target Earnings (OTE): $280,000 - $350,000
- Base Salary: 50% of OTE
- Uncapped Commission
- Accelerators for over-achievement (2x+ accelerators above 120%)
- Equity: Stock options package
- 401(k) with company match
- Full health, dental, vision coverage
- Unlimited PTO
- Home office stipend
- President's Club incentive trip

Territory: Western United States (CA, WA, OR, AZ, CO, NV)
Location: Remote within territory, with travel to San Francisco HQ monthly
