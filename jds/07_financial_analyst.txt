Job Title: Senior Financial Analyst - Corporate FP&A

Company: TechCorp Industries

About TechCorp Industries:
TechCorp Industries is a publicly traded (NASDAQ: TECH) diversified technology company with $2.5B in annual revenue. We operate across three business segments: Enterprise Software, Cloud Services, and Hardware Solutions. Our company has grown 25% year-over-year and we're looking to strengthen our Finance team to support continued expansion.

Role Overview:
The Senior Financial Analyst will be a key member of our Corporate FP&A team, supporting strategic financial planning, budgeting, forecasting, and executive reporting. This role partners closely with business unit leaders and C-suite executives to drive data-driven decision making.

Key Responsibilities:

Financial Planning & Analysis:
- Lead the annual budgeting process for assigned business units ($500M+ revenue)
- Prepare quarterly forecasts and variance analysis against budget and prior periods
- Build and maintain complex financial models for scenario planning and sensitivity analysis
- Develop long-range financial plans (3-5 years) aligned with corporate strategy
- Analyze key financial metrics including revenue growth, margins, CAC, LTV, and unit economics

Reporting & Presentations:
- Prepare monthly financial packages for executive leadership and Board of Directors
- Create compelling presentations that translate complex financial data into actionable insights
- Support quarterly earnings preparation including investor presentations and Q&A materials
- Develop automated dashboards and reports using Tableau and Power BI

Business Partnership:
- Serve as finance business partner to Cloud Services division leadership
- Evaluate ROI for capital investments and strategic initiatives
- Support M&A due diligence and integration planning
- Partner with Accounting on month-end close and revenue recognition analysis

Process Improvement:
- Identify opportunities to streamline FP&A processes and improve efficiency
- Lead implementation of new planning tools and technologies
- Document best practices and train junior team members

Required Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, Economics, or related field
- 5-7 years of progressive experience in FP&A, corporate finance, or investment banking
- Advanced Excel skills (pivot tables, VLOOKUP, INDEX/MATCH, financial modeling)
- Experience with ERP systems (SAP, Oracle, NetSuite)
- Proficiency in financial planning tools (Adaptive Insights, Anaplan, or Hyperion)
- Strong understanding of GAAP and financial statements
- Experience working in a public company environment preferred

Preferred Qualifications:
- MBA or Master's degree in Finance
- CFA or CPA designation
- Experience in technology or SaaS industry
- SQL skills for data extraction and analysis
- Experience with Tableau, Power BI, or similar visualization tools

Skills & Attributes:
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Excellent written and verbal communication
- Ability to manage multiple priorities and meet deadlines
- Detail-oriented with commitment to accuracy
- Proactive self-starter who takes ownership
- Collaborative team player

Compensation & Benefits:
- Base Salary: $130,000 - $160,000
- Annual Bonus: 15% target
- Equity: RSU grant valued at $40,000
- 401(k) with 6% company match
- Comprehensive health benefits
- Flexible work arrangement (hybrid 2-3 days in office)
- Professional development support
- Employee stock purchase plan (15% discount)

Location: Austin, Texas (Hybrid)
