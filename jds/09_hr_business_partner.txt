Job Title: HR Business Partner

Company: GlobalTech Solutions

About GlobalTech Solutions:
GlobalTech Solutions is a leading enterprise software company with 3,500 employees across North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific. We provide cloud-based solutions for supply chain management and have been recognized as a "Best Place to Work" for five consecutive years. We're currently undergoing significant growth and transformation.

Role Summary:
We are seeking a strategic HR Business Partner to support our Engineering and Product organizations (approximately 800 employees). This role serves as a trusted advisor to senior leadership, driving people strategies that enable business success. You will partner with executives to address complex organizational challenges while ensuring alignment with company culture and values.

Key Responsibilities:

Strategic Partnership:
- Serve as strategic HR partner to VP of Engineering and VP of Product
- Participate in leadership meetings and contribute to business strategy discussions
- Translate business objectives into HR priorities and initiatives
- Provide data-driven insights to inform people decisions
- Lead organizational design and workforce planning efforts

Talent Management:
- Partner with leaders on performance management, including calibration sessions
- Identify high-potential employees and develop succession plans
- Coach managers on development conversations and career pathing
- Support talent review processes and retention strategies
- Drive adoption of continuous feedback culture

Employee Relations:
- Handle complex employee relations matters with discretion and professionalism
- Conduct investigations and recommend appropriate actions
- Advise managers on performance improvement plans and terminations
- Ensure compliance with employment laws and company policies
- Mediate conflicts and facilitate difficult conversations

Change Management:
- Lead HR aspects of organizational restructuring and team changes
- Support M&A integration activities related to people and culture
- Champion change initiatives and help employees navigate transitions
- Develop communication strategies for sensitive organizational changes

Compensation & Analytics:
- Partner with Total Rewards on compensation decisions and market analysis
- Analyze HR metrics (turnover, engagement, headcount) and present findings
- Use data to identify trends and recommend interventions
- Support annual compensation review and promotion cycles

Required Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business, Psychology, or related field
- 7+ years of progressive HR experience, with 3+ years as an HRBP
- Experience supporting technology or engineering organizations
- Strong knowledge of employment law and HR compliance
- Experience with HRIS systems (Workday preferred)
- Demonstrated ability to influence senior leaders
- Track record of successfully managing complex employee relations issues

Preferred Qualifications:
- Master's degree in HR, Organizational Development, or MBA
- SHRM-SCP, SPHR, or similar certification
- Experience in high-growth technology companies
- Experience supporting global teams and remote workforce
- Change management certification (Prosci or similar)
- Background in organizational development or HR analytics

Core Competencies:
- Business Acumen: Understands business operations and financial drivers
- Strategic Thinking: Connects HR initiatives to business outcomes
- Influencing Skills: Builds credibility and influences without authority
- Emotional Intelligence: Navigates sensitive situations with empathy
- Analytical Skills: Uses data to drive decisions and measure impact
- Communication: Articulates complex information clearly at all levels

Compensation & Benefits:
- Base Salary: $125,000 - $155,000
- Annual Bonus: 15% target
- Equity: RSU grant
- Comprehensive health, dental, vision coverage
- 401(k) with company match
- Flexible work arrangements (remote-friendly)
- Unlimited PTO
- Parental leave (16 weeks)
- Professional development budget ($3,000/year)
- Employee assistance program

Location: San Francisco Bay Area (Hybrid - 2 days in office) or Remote
