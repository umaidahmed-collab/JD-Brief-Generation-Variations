**QUESTIONS FOR YOU:**

**About the Company & Team:**
1. What is the company culture like? (e.g., formal/informal, collaborative, fast-paced, etc.)
2. What is the size and structure of the HR team? Who will this person report to?
3. What is the current structure of the Engineering and Product organizations? (e.g., number of teams, leadership structure)
4. Are there any recent or upcoming changes (e.g., mergers, acquisitions, rapid growth) that the HRBP should be aware of?
5. What are the main challenges or priorities facing the Engineering and Product teams right now?

**About the Role:**
6. Why is this position open? (e.g., backfill, new role, internal promotion)
7. What are the top 3-5 priorities for this role in the first 6-12 months?
8. What does success look like in this role? (How will you measure it?)
9. Who are the key stakeholders this person will interact with most frequently?
10. Are there any travel expectations? (If so, how much and where?)

**Candidate Profile:**
11. Are you open to candidates from outside the tech industry if they have strong HRBP experience?
12. Is experience with global teams a must-have, or just nice-to-have?
13. Is experience with Workday (HRIS) required, or will other systems suffice?
14. Are you open to candidates who require visa sponsorship?
15. What are the absolute deal-breakers (must-haves) for this role?
16. Are there any soft skills or personality traits that are especially important?
17. Are there any companies or industries you’d prefer to target or avoid?

**Compensation & Logistics:**
18. Is the salary range flexible for the right candidate?
19. Is there a preferred start date?
20. Is the hybrid/remote policy strict, or is there flexibility?

**Interview Process:**
21. What does the interview process look like? (e.g., number of rounds, panel interviews, assessments)
22. Who will be involved in the interviews?
23. Are there any assessments or case studies as part of the process?

**Anything Else:**
24. Is there anything else you want the recruiter to know to help them find the best candidates?
