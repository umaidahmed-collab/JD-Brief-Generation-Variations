## Questions for a Comprehensive Recruiter Brief

### 1. **About the Company**
- What does your company do? (Describe in simple terms, e.g., "We make software for banks," or "We are an e-commerce company.")
- How big is your company? (Number of employees, size of tech team, etc.)
- What is the company culture like? (Formal/casual, fast-paced/steady, collaborative/independent, etc.)
- Where is your company located? Is this role remote, hybrid, or on-site? If hybrid/on-site, where is the office?

### 2. **About the Team & Reporting**
- Which team will this person join? (E.g., QA team, product team, etc.)
- Who will they report to? (E.g., QA Manager, CTO, etc.)
- How big is the immediate team? (Number of QA Engineers, Developers, etc.)
- Are there any direct reports or is this an individual contributor role?

### 3. **About the Role**
- Why is this role open? (New headcount, replacement, etc.)
- What are the main day-to-day responsibilities? (Highlight the most frequent tasks.)
- What are the most important outcomes you expect from this person in the first 6-12 months?
- Are there any specific products/projects this person will work on?
- What is the tech stack they’ll be testing? (E.g., web apps, mobile apps, APIs, etc.)

### 4. **Skills & Experience**
- Which skills/technologies are **must-haves**? (Please prioritize if possible.)
- Which skills/technologies are **nice-to-have** but not required?
- Are there any certifications that are required or strongly preferred?
- What level of experience is required? (Minimum years, seniority, etc.)
- Are you open to candidates from outside your industry?
- Is experience with any particular tools or platforms (e.g., Selenium, Jenkins, AWS) critical?

### 5. **Soft Skills & Competencies**
- What are the most important soft skills for success in this role? (E.g., communication, attention to detail, teamwork, independence, etc.)
- Are there any particular personality traits or work styles that fit best with your team/company?

### 6. **Logistics & Compensation**
- What is the salary range and benefits package for this role?
- Are you open to candidates in different locations/time zones?
- What is the expected start date?
- Any visa sponsorship available or required?

### 7. **Recruitment Process**
- What is the interview process? (Steps, assessments, who will be involved, etc.)
- Any specific instructions for the recruiter about CV screening, questions to ask, red flags, etc.?
