### 2. **Role Overview**

The **Senior Financial Analyst – Corporate FP&A** (Financial Planning & Analysis) is a key member of the finance team. This person will:
- Help plan the company’s finances
- Analyze financial data to help executives make decisions
- Work with leaders of different business areas (especially Cloud Services)
- Prepare reports and presentations for top executives and the Board of Directors

**FP&A** means Financial Planning & Analysis. It involves budgeting, forecasting, analyzing company performance, and supporting strategic decision-making.

---

### 3. **Key Responsibilities (What will this person do?)**

**A. Financial Planning & Analysis**
- **Budgeting:** Lead the annual budget process for business areas with at least $500 million in revenue.
- **Forecasting:** Prepare quarterly forecasts (predicting future results) and explain differences between actual results and budget.
- **Financial Modeling:** Build and maintain detailed spreadsheets to test different business scenarios (“what if” analysis).
- **Long-Range Planning:** Help create 3-5 year financial plans in line with company strategy.
- **Metric Analysis:** Analyze important financial numbers, such as:
    - **Revenue growth:** How fast the company is growing sales.
    - **Margins:** Profitability.
    - **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** Cost to get a new customer.
    - **LTV (Lifetime Value):** How much revenue a customer brings over time.
    - **Unit Economics:** Profitability per product or customer.

**B. Reporting & Presentations**
- **Executive Reports:** Prepare monthly financial reports for company leaders and the Board.
- **Presentations:** Create clear, visually appealing presentations to explain financial data to non-finance executives.
- **Earnings Support:** Help prepare materials for quarterly earnings calls with investors.
- **Dashboards:** Build automated dashboards (visual reports) using tools like Tableau and Power BI.

**C. Business Partnership**
- **Business Partner:** Work closely with Cloud Services division leaders to help them make financial decisions.
- **Investment Analysis:** Evaluate the return on investment (ROI) for projects and company initiatives.
- **M&A Support:** Assist with financial analysis for company acquisitions and integration of new businesses.
- **Accounting Collaboration:** Work with the accounting team on closing the books each month and analyzing revenue.

**D. Process Improvement**
- **Efficiency:** Find ways to make finance processes faster and more accurate.
- **Technology:** Lead the rollout of new finance software/tools.
- **Best Practices:** Document and share best practices; train junior team members.
