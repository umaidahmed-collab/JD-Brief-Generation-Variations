## Questions for the Comprehensive Recruiter Brief

### 1. About Your Company
- What does your company do (industry, products/services, size, location)?
- Who are your main customers or stakeholders (government, private sector, public, etc.)?
- What is the company culture like (formal/informal, fast-paced/steady, collaborative/independent, etc.)?
- Is there any recent news, growth, or context about the company relevant to this hire?

### 2. About the Team
- How large is the Applications Service Management team? What roles are in the team?
- Who will this person report to (name and job title)?
- Who will report to this person (how many direct/indirect reports, and their types of roles)?
- How does this team interact with other departments? (For example: IT Infrastructure, Security, Business Users, etc.)

### 3. About the Role
- Why is this role open? (New position, replacement, reorganization, etc.)
- What are the three most important goals for this person in the first 12 months?
- What are the top 3 biggest challenges they will face?
- Is this a hands-on leadership role, or more strategic/overseeing?
- Will the person be expected to work onsite, hybrid, or remote? Where is the main office located?
- Are there any travel expectations for this role (between offices, to vendor sites, etc.)?

### 4. Technical Requirements (Explain in Plain English)
- Which ERP system(s) do you use (e.g., SAP S/4HANA, Oracle, Microsoft Dynamics)? Are there other key enterprise applications they must manage?
- Is experience with a specific ERP system essential, or are you open to candidates with Tier 1 ERP experience generally?
- Are your systems hosted “on-premises” (in your data center), in the “cloud” (like AWS, Azure), or a mix (“hybrid”)? Please explain.
- Which service management tools are currently used (e.g., ServiceNow, Jira Service Management, Zendesk)? Is experience with any essential?
- What are the most important technical skills or knowledge areas for this role (please list in plain English)?

### 5. Leadership & Soft Skills
- What kind of leadership style works best in your organization? (e.g., hands-on, mentoring, directive, collaborative)
- What are the most important soft skills for success in this role (e.g., communication, influencing, resilience)?
- Are there any key internal or external stakeholders this person must be able to build strong relationships with? (For example, senior government officials, vendors, business leaders)

### 6. Education & Certification Preferences
- Is a bachelor’s degree mandatory? Would equivalent experience be considered?
- Is a master’s degree (MBA or MSc) required, or just a “nice to have”?
- Is ITIL v4 certification required or preferred?

### 7. Compensation & Benefits (for context)
- What is the salary range and bonus/benefits structure for this role (if available)?
- Are there any specific benefits or perks that make this role/company attractive?

### 8. Candidate Fit & Dealbreakers
- What experience or qualities are absolutely essential (your “dealbreakers”)?
- What experience or qualities are “nice to have,” but not essential?
- Are there any backgrounds or types of candidates you do **not** want to see (e.g., only from small companies, not enough leadership, etc.)?

### 9. Recruitment Process
- What is your preferred interview process (how many stages, who will interview, any assessments/tests)?
- What is your target start date for this role?
