### 1. **About the Company**

### 2. **About the Team & Reporting Structure**
- Who will the QA Engineer report to (job title)?
- How big is the current QA or development team?
- Who will the QA Engineer work closely with (e.g., developers, product managers)?
- Are there other QA team members, or will this person be the only one?
- What is the style of management (hands-on, collaborative, independent)?

### 3. **About the Role**
- Is this a brand-new position or a replacement hire?
- Why is this position open (growth, backfill, new projects)?
- What are the top 3-5 priorities for the first 6 months in this role?
- What does success look like in the first year?
- What challenges should a candidate be aware of?

### 4. **Role Logistics**
- Is this position remote, hybrid, or onsite? If onsite/hybrid, where is the office located?
- What are the working hours and any flexibility (e.g., standard hours, shift work, time zones)?
- Is there a preference for candidates based in a specific location or country?
- Is sponsorship or visa assistance available for the right candidate?

### 5. **Technical Stack & Tools**
- What programming languages and frameworks does your team use?
- Which test automation tools do you currently use (e.g., Selenium, Cypress, Playwright)? Any plans to change?
- What CI/CD tools do you use (e.g., Jenkins, GitLab CI)?
- What databases and scripting languages are most relevant day-to-day?
- Are there any proprietary or uncommon tools/processes the candidate should know about?
- How much of the testing is manual vs. automated currently?

### 6. **Must-Have vs. Nice-to-Have Skills**
- Of all the skills listed, which ones are absolutely essential?
- Are there any skills or certifications that are “nice to have” but not mandatory?
- Is experience with cloud platforms or API testing required, or just a bonus?

### 7. **Soft Skills & Culture Fit**
- What personal qualities do you value most (e.g., independence, collaboration, curiosity)?
- Are there any particular communication or teamwork skills that are especially important for this team?

### 8. **Interview Process**
- What is your typical interview process (steps, assessments, etc.)?
- Who will be involved in the interviews?
- Are there any specific assessments or coding tests?
- How soon do you need someone to start?

### 9. **Compensation & Benefits**
- What is the salary range for this role?
- Are there any bonuses, equity, or other incentives?
- What benefits are offered (healthcare, remote work, learning budget, etc.)?

### 10. **Other Notes**
- Are there any red flags or dealbreakers?
- Anything else you want the recruiter to communicate to candidates?
